Japan overtime additional payment calculator

For employees whose base salary includes a fixed 45-hour overtime allowance

How this works: Your base salary already covers up to 45 hours of overtime per month. Additional payment is only triggered if you work more than 45 overtime hours, work between 10pm–5am, or work on a statutory holiday (Sunday or year-end / new year days).

Saturday and public holidays are not statutory holidays — hours worked on those days count as regular overtime toward your 45-hour total.
